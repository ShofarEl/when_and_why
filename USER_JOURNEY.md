# User Journey - Research Study Participation

## Overview
This document describes the complete user journey for participants in the AI-assisted creativity research study, from initial consent through study completion.

## Study Flow - Step by Step

### 1. **Landing & Consent (5-10 minutes)**
- **Welcome Screen**: Participants arrive at the study landing page
- **Consent Form**: Detailed informed consent with:
  - Study purpose and procedures
  - Data collection and privacy information
  - AI system usage explanation
  - Risks, benefits, and participant rights
  - Contact information for questions
- **Consent Decision**: Participants must actively consent to proceed
- **Participant ID Assignment**: System generates unique pseudonymous ID

### 2. **Pre-Study Survey (10 minutes)**
- **Demographics Collection**:
  - Age (18-100)
  - Academic level (Bachelor's, Master's, PhD, Other)
  - Major/Program of study
- **Experience Assessment**:
  - Data science familiarity (1-7 scale)
  - AI tools experience (1-7 scale)
  - Prior relevant coursework (checklist)

### 3. **Tutorial & Practice (5 minutes)**
- **Interface Introduction**: Overview of the task interface
- **AI Assistant Demo**: How to interact with AI helper (if applicable)
- **Practice Task**: Short practice round to familiarize with system
- **Condition Assignment**: System randomly assigns to experimental condition

### 4. **Main Experimental Tasks (40 minutes)**
Participants complete 4 creative problem-framing tasks:

#### Task Structure (10 minutes each):
- **Dataset Presentation**: Brief description of a real-world dataset
- **Problem Framing**: Generate research questions and hypotheses
- **AI Interaction** (condition-dependent):
  - **Control**: No AI assistance
  - **Just-in-Time**: AI help available on request
  - **Always-On**: Continuous AI suggestions
- **Reflection**: Brief rationale for final choices
- **Post-Task Questions**: Quick ratings and feedback

#### Example Datasets:
1. Student performance and engagement data
2. Urban mobility and transportation patterns
3. Social media behavior and mental health indicators
4. Environmental sensors and air quality measurements

### 5. **Transfer Tasks (7 minutes)**
- **Novel Scenarios**: 2 new datasets without AI assistance
- **Knowledge Application**: Test transfer of learned problem-framing skills
- **Comparison Metrics**: Assess improvement from main tasks

### 6. **Post-Study Survey (3 minutes)**
- **Experience Ratings**:
  - Task difficulty and engagement
  - AI helpfulness (if applicable)
  - Perceived learning and creativity
- **Preference Rankings**: Compare different assistance approaches
- **Open Feedback**: Comments and suggestions
- **Participant ID Display**: For potential data deletion requests

### 7. **Completion & Debriefing (2 minutes)**
- **Thank You Message**: Appreciation for participation
- **Study Purpose Reveal**: Brief explanation of research goals
- **Contact Information**: How to reach researchers with questions
- **Data Deletion Reminder**: 30-day window for deletion requests
- **Participant ID Reminder**: Save for potential future contact

## Total Time Commitment
- **Estimated Duration**: 60 minutes
- **Actual Range**: 45-75 minutes (based on individual pace)
- **Break Policy**: Participants can pause at any time

## Technical Requirements
- **Browser**: Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- **Internet**: Stable connection for AI interactions
- **Device**: Desktop, laptop, or tablet (mobile not recommended)
- **JavaScript**: Must be enabled

## Data Collection Points

### Automatically Recorded:
- Task responses and timestamps
- AI interaction logs (prompts and outputs)
- Survey responses and ratings
- Session metadata (completion times, condition)

### Not Collected:
- Personal identifiers (names, emails, IPs)
- Device fingerprints or tracking data
- Keystroke patterns or mouse movements
- Any information outside the study interface

## Participant Rights & Support

### During the Study:
- **Voluntary Participation**: Can withdraw at any time
- **Break Flexibility**: Pause whenever needed
- **Technical Support**: Contact information provided
- **Privacy Protection**: No personal information required

### After Completion:
- **Data Deletion**: 30-day window with participant ID
- **Questions**: Researcher contact available
- **Results Access**: Aggregated findings will be published
- **Follow-up**: No future contact unless requested

## Quality Assurance

### Data Validation:
- Required field completion
- Response time monitoring
- Attention check questions
- Technical error logging

### Participant Experience:
- Clear instructions at each step
- Progress indicators throughout
- Responsive design for different devices
- Error handling and recovery

## Ethical Considerations

### Privacy Protection:
- Pseudonymous data collection only
- Secure encrypted data transmission
- Limited researcher access
- Clear data retention policies

### Informed Consent:
- Comprehensive information disclosure
- Voluntary agreement confirmation
- Right to withdraw emphasized
- Contact information provided

### Risk Mitigation:
- Minimal personal information collection
- Clear warnings about AI interactions
- Secure data handling procedures
- Transparent research purposes

## Expected Outcomes

### For Participants:
- Experience with AI-assisted problem framing
- Exposure to real-world data science scenarios
- Contribution to educational research
- Potential learning about research question development

### For Research:
- Understanding of AI assistance timing effects
- Insights into creativity and learning patterns
- Data on human-AI collaboration preferences
- Evidence for educational tool design

## Post-Study Data Handling

### Immediate (0-30 days):
- Individual data deletion possible
- Raw data analysis begins
- Quality checks and validation

### Medium-term (1-6 months):
- Statistical analysis completion
- Aggregated results preparation
- Individual deletion still possible

### Long-term (6+ months):
- Publication preparation
- Anonymized dataset creation
- Individual deletion may become impossible

This user journey ensures a comprehensive, ethical, and scientifically rigorous research experience while protecting participant privacy and rights.
